About Our School

Our History

Sunrise Academy opened its doors in 1998 with a commitment to academic excellence, inclusive leadership, and community engagement. Over two decades, the school has grown into a modern, student-centered campus where curiosity and creativity thrive.

Mission

To empower every learner with the knowledge, skills, and character needed to succeed in a changing world.

Vision

To be a leading school that inspires confident thinkers, compassionate citizens, and lifelong learners.

Core Values

  • Respect and integrity
  • Curiosity and innovation
  • Teamwork and resilience
  • Community service

Facilities
