Principal's Welcome

Message from the Principal

Welcome to Sunrise Academy, where every student is encouraged to explore ideas, build confidence, and celebrate achievement. We cultivate a supportive learning community that values creativity, critical thinking, and kindness.

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum blends strong core academics with hands-on learning to help students excel in math, science, language arts, and social studies.

Student Well-being

We prioritize social-emotional growth with counseling, character education, and positive classroom experiences.

Community Engagement

Families, teachers, and students collaborate through service projects, events, and student-led activities.
