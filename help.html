Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply?

Visit the Admission page and complete the online form. Submit your application after entering the required student and guardian details.

What are the admission requirements?

Applicants must provide a completed registration form, proof of age, previous school records when applicable, and guardian contact information.

What grades are offered?

Sunrise Academy offers Kindergarten through Grade 12 with programs designed for early learners, middle school, and high school students.

How do I contact the school?

Use the email address or phone number listed below, or visit the school office during normal school hours.

What are the school hours?

School hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM. Early care begins at 7:30 AM, and after-school clubs continue until 5:00 PM.
